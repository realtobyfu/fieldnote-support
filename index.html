About

Fieldnote is your personal herbarium — a place to document, organize, and learn from your encounters with plants and trees. Whether you're a seasoned botanist or just beginning to notice the green world around you, Fieldnote helps you capture and remember every discovery.

Capture — Photograph plants and identify them instantly with AI

Library — Build your personal collection of observed species

Explore — Discover new plants waiting to be found nearby

FAQ

Does Fieldnote work offline?

Yes! Your entire plant library is stored locally on your device. You can browse, edit, and add manual entries without an internet connection. AI identification requires connectivity.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your photos and observations stay on your device. We don't collect personal data or track your location history. The app syncs via iCloud for your personal backup only.

How accurate is the plant identification?

Our AI provides a confidence score with each identification. We encourage you to verify identifications, especially for plants you plan to consume or handle. Every identification is a learning opportunity!

Can I export my data?

Your data syncs automatically via iCloud to your other Apple devices. Full export features are coming in a future update.
